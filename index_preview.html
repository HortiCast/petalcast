Garden care driven by your weather

PetalCast is an intelligent system that pairs hyper-local weather tracking with a complete map of your garden. By understanding your specific postcode, spaces, and plant ages, it predicts exact needs and sends precise care alerts right when they matter.

How it works

1

Your weather dictates the care

PetalCast monitors your exact postcode continuously — not a generic national forecast. It tracks rainfall, temperature, and sunlight down to the hour. If it rains on Wednesday, your watering alerts automatically shift to Thursday.

2

Map your microclimates

Describe your unique spaces — whether it's a north-facing border, a windy balcony, or a shady side passage. PetalCast builds a living map of your garden's distinct zones and conditions, not just a flat checklist.

3

Deep plant profiles

Log your plants by searching, typing, or photographing a whole border. Tell PetalCast the maturity and age of your plants so the system can adjust its logic — because a newly planted shrub needs entirely different care than an established one.

4

Smart, localised alerts

Receive plain-English nudges tailored to specific locations. "Time to water the dry shade bed — pay extra attention to the young hydrangeas." No static schedules. Just intelligent, real-time guidance based on your garden's actual state.
